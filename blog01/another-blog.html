Kakashi Hatake

The Copy Ninja of the Hidden Leaf.

Kakashi Hatake’s story is one of wisdom, strength, and mystery. Known as the Copy Ninja, Kakashi’s journey is filled with lessons of leadership, sacrifice, and the importance of teamwork. His calm demeanor and unmatched skills make him one of the most iconic characters in the anime world.
